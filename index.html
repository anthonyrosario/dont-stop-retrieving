Don't Stop Retrieving is an awesome web app that can access any off leash park of your wildest dreams in Seattle, Washington. There are many great places in Seattle to take your dogs, there are more dogs in Seattle than children because we love dogs! Come one, come all, enjoy a free off leash dog park. Enter your address to locate the closest parks to you. Woof! You can look at each specific park and obtain hours of operation and more. It is even possible to add reviews to the park and see what other Seattleites are saying about that specific dog park that you're looking at! Thank you Seattle for investing in our parks and as always, please respect the parks, other dogs, and other dog owners!
